Tell me your idea and let's make it happen.

Bird house kits were made and sent out to assemble and paint.

During the pandemic I wanted to help my grandkids, keep them entertained. My did make a lot of birdhouses. Scrap lumber became bird house kits. Grandkids, neighbor kids, the mailman's 5 boys. The last step on instructions was to send us a photo of the finished project.

Scarey!

For some halloween fun, we invited the granddaughters over to make scrarecrows. Stick poles and straw would limit them, so forms were made so the arms and legs could be moved as needed. The girls had a great day assembling the forms, getting them dressed and set up on the lawns.

Art for the holiday.

Put a little love in your heart! Show your valentine some love with a set of hearts!

Then when winter comes, add some fun flakes! Finish to suit your decor. Sit on a shelf or mount on the wall.

Plinko.

My daughter asked me to build a Plinko game for a fundraiser. After assembling, my granddaughters tested the game, noting how ofter the ball landed in each slot. Then we tweeked the pins to level out the odds.
