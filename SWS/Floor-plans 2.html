Plans make life easier.

A well drawn floor plan is key to a beautiful outcome. I can deliver drawings to scale that your contractor, builder and cabinet maker can use to complete your project. In the design process we dig into your current layout and ask key questions to get us to your final desired floorplan.

  • Where are the load bearing walls?
  • Which walls do you want to eliminte?
  • Where are the furnace ductwork?
  • Can the ductwork be relocated?
  • Where are electrical feeds?
